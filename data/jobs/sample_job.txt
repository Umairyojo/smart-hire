Associate Software Developer

We are seeking a talented and motivated Associate Software Developer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in Java and SQL, experience with web technologies, and a passion for creating innovative, scalable solutions. As an Associate Software Developer, you will contribute to various projects, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and play a key role in delivering impactful results.

Responsibilities:

Develop and customize software modules, collaborating with a team to implement new features and handle system migrations.

Lead project development, including building comprehensive domain sites and managing client communications.

Create API integrations to improve data management efficiency.

Optimize site performance, ensuring high availability and seamless user experiences.

Contribute to all phases of the development lifecycle, from design to deployment and maintenance.

Required Skills and Experience:

1 year of experience as an Associate Software Developer or similar role (2022-2023). 


Core Java: Strong understanding of OOPS concepts (Inheritance, Polymorphism, Encapsulation, Abstraction), Object class, Multi-Threading, and Exception Handling. 


SQL: Proficient in writing SQL queries, DDL, DML, and TCL commands, with good knowledge of Sub-queries, Co-related Sub-queries, and Joins. 


Web Technologies: Experience with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. 


Drupal 9 CMS: Knowledge of PHP, jQuery, Ajax, custom module creation, and migration. 


Problem-solving and Analytical Skills: Strong ability to analyze problems and devise effective solutions. 


Collaboration and Communication: Excellent interpersonal skills for teamwork and client interaction. 



Adaptability: Quick to learn and adapt to new technologies. 


Time Management: Effective in managing time and meeting deadlines. 

Preferred Qualifications:

Experience with Oracle 10g database, workbench, or mysqlite. 

Familiarity with Library Management System concepts.
